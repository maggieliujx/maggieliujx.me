Instagram is a social media platform that allows users to share content, explore and connect with each other.

PROPOSAL: INSTAGRAM EXPLORE

An addition to the current collection feature that allows users to look at related content on the explore page, based on content in those collections.

Team
Ashley Wu
My Roles
User Experience
User Research
Prototyping
Interface Design
User Interaction
Duration
3 week feature project for a senior interface design course (spring 2018)

Project Context

We were to design an additional feature to an existing mobile application.

Client Context

Instagram uses an algorithm to generate content based on the content users interact with: likes, comments, searches, follows.

Opportunity

Through our user research, we found that the algorithm in the explore page could be too sensitive, or would generate similar content that the user accidentally clicked on but didn't want to see. We saw this as an opportunity to further engage users with the platform, by giving them control over what they see when they want to see them.

“Instagram exists to do one thing: Maximize the time you spend using Instagram.”
– Medium, 2017

Target Audience

User Flow

Instagram's current collection feature.

Our Proposal

After finalizing the placement and visual design of the feature, my teammate took to prototyping it, and I finished with the interactions of the feature.

 

We adapted Instagram's current interactions of switching between screens:

1. swiping left and right in the post section 2. tapping the collection name at the top

Reflection

It was quite challenging working with Instagram as a client, especially since our proposal relies entirely on their algorithm. There were limited resources on how their algorithm actually works, so we were careful to not assume we can change it, instead utilize it in another way.
